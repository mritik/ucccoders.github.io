The Two Sections

Our club covers two broad aspects of coding: Algorithm Study and Project Development.

Algorithms and Competitions

Run by Kevin Liu

During each section discussion, Kevin will first give a short introduction on a well-defined algorithmic topic. Then he will leave a typical problem for the topic for members to work over the next couple of weeks. At the next meeting, Kevin will first allow members to share their solutions for the problem before moving onto another topic. After a topic is done, a summary note and a solution to the previous weekly problem will be posted. In the weeks leading up to the CCC, the main focus will be weekly CCC problems to help members prepare.

Game and Web Development

Run by William Trojniak

Every two months or so, William will be presenting a general theme around which the club members will design a game, website or similar project individually, focusing on a certain skill each meeting. These skills could include techniques such as database management, game design, and etc. By the end of the two months, the goal for the club members is that by incorporating these elements into their own projects, they have created something fully functional with their own creative takes on the original theme.
