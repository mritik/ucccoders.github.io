The Algorithms Section: What Do We Do?

The Algorithms Section at the UCC Coding and Algorithms Academy, led by student Kevin Liu, meets once every two weeks to discuss an algorithms-related concept, with an aim to spark interest in computer algorithms as well as their applications in the real-world or in contest problems.
